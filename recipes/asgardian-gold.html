Asgardian Gold

Experience the epic intensity of Ragnarok Roast.

Ingredients

  • Asgardian Gold coffee beans
  • Filtered water

Recipe

  1. Start by grinding Asgardian Gold coffee beans to a medium consistency.
  2. Measure 2 tablespoons of ground coffee per 6 ounces of filtered water.
  3. Bring the filtered water to a boil and let it cool slightly for a moment.
  4. Place the ground coffee into your preferred brewing device, such as a drip coffee maker or a French press.
  5. Slowly pour the hot water over the coffee grounds, ensuring they are fully saturated.
  6. Let the coffee brew for approximately 4-5 minutes, allowing the flavors to fully develop.
  7. If using a French press, gently press the plunger down to separate the coffee grounds from the brewed coffee. If using a drip coffee maker, let the brewing process complete.
  8. Pour the Asgardian Gold coffee into your favorite mug or cup.
  9. Optionally, you can add sweetener, milk, or cream according to your taste preference.
  10. Take a moment to savor the enchanting golden brew of Asgardian Gold, and enjoy the rich flavors it offers.
