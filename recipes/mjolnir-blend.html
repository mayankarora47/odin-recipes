Mjolnir Blend

Unleash the power of flavor with Mjolnir Blend

Ingredients

  • Mjolnir Blend coffee beans
  • Filtered water

Recipe

  1. Add 2 tablespoons of ground coffee per 6 ounces of filtered water in a coffee maker or French press.
  2. Brew for 4-5 minutes.
  3. Serve and enjoy the mighty flavor of Mjolnir Blend.
